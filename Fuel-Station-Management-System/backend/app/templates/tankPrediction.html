How It Works

  • Select the tank you want to monitor from the dropdown.
  • Enter the current fuel level from your dipstick or sensor reading.
  • The ML model analyses historical consumption to predict depletion.
  • Results show estimated days remaining and the projected empty date.
  • Schedule refills before the critical threshold is reached.
